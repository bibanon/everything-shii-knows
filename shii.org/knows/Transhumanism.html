Examples of high transhumanist thought

Wouldn't it be great if:

  • we had wings?
  • we had X-ray vision?
  • we could heal ourselves like Wolverine?
  • we were furries?
  • we were coddled brains in vats?

Examples of intelligent transhumanist debate

  • "You know what? If you don't like human enhancement that's only because you've been educated stupid by our Puritan society. Deal with it!"
    • Typically said to no-one at all.
  • "The high muckity-muck said that immortality would either happen in our lifetime or not happen at all."
    • This is a form of rapture belief. Transhumanists will typically refuse to recognize that it is a rapture belief because they think they're too smart for religion.

Quotes

Actually, come to think of it, the parallels between Transhumanism ca. 1990-2008 and Marxism 1880-1930 are pretty striking. Confident talk about "undeniable" facts; the obsession with inventing historical laws and postulating a firm path for future history; the casual dismissal of previous systems of thought as obsolete; the development of an increasingly arcane vocabulary with an increasingly tenuous connection to lived experience; fantasies of warping time and space on a fundamental level (see Aleksey Gastev for an example of how this played out in Marxist thinking); ideological strategies designed to mark off certain populations as obsolete or reactionary; the ideological reification of "science." Let's hope the Transhumanists never have their 1917.
Nasreddin, Metafilter
