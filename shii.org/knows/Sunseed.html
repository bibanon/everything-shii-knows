Sunseed is a 1973 movie featuring religious gurus-- in other words, a lot like Aliens from Spaceship Earth. http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0948127/

People appearing in Sunseed

Mentioned in title:

  • Sri Bhagavan
  • Lama Anagarika Govinda
  • Maharaji Virsa Singh
  • Yogi Bhajan
  • Sri Brim Dass
  • Swami Satchidananda
  • Swami Muktananda
  • Baba Ram Dass
  • Rabbi Schlomo Carlebach
  • Pir Vilayat Inayat Khan
  • "And Others Known and Unknown"

But the movie actually spends a fair amount of time following around an utterly despicable person named Murshid Sam.
