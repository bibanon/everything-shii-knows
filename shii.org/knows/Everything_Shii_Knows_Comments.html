You're reading Everything Shii Knows, a wiki of personal opinion. I welcome your comments, questions, and criticism. It will be especially useful to me if you write your comments in such a way that I can respond to them on the wiki page, but if you'd like to write a personal message that's fine too.

  • E-mail: shii@shii.org . Start your letters with "Dear Shii" for maximum spam filter avoidance.
  • Replies should take a couple of days under normal circumstances
  • Letters may be posted on wiki articles but will be appreciated, never ridiculed!
