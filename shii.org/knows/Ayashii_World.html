Ayashii World ("strange world"), once the biggest underground site in Japan, consists of a group of anonymous bulletin board systems. It is often written as ayashii World. Its original founder is SHIBA Masayuki (username: "Shiba"). It is considered to be the origin of underground sites on the Japanese Internet.
