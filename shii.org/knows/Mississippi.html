I am very knowledgeable about Mississippi. I visited there for a whole week! Things to know about Mississippi:

  • They eat crawfish there! You tear off the tail and suck out its guts.
  • There are a lot of churches.
  • There are some genuine rednecks there and they're funny guys!
  • A lot of people moved there from Alabama because Mississippi is twice as intelligent as Alabama, or something.
  • Even if they don't think they have Southern accents, they really do!
  • Some of it looks a lot like every other place in the United States and some of it looks like everything you remember from To Kill a Mockingbird. Including the racists.
