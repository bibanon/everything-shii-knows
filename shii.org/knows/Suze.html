White Negroni (Pegu)

  • 1.5 parts Suze
  • 3 parts gin
  • 2 parts Lillet wine

Suze extrême

  • 1/3 Suze
  • 1/4 frozen gin

Suze ensoleillée

  • 1/3 Suze
  • 1/3 orange juice

Suze sensuelle

  • 2/3 Suze
  • 1/3 blackcurrant sorbet

Suze florida

  • 1 part Suze
  • 2 part grapefruit juice
  • sugar

Suze mojito

  • 2 parts Suze
  • 3 parts champagne
  • 20 drops of Angostura bitters
  • spoonful of sugar
  • lemon juice
  • mint

Suze insolite

  • 1 part Suze
  • 2 parts orange juice
  • 1 part crème de cassis (blackcurrant wine)
  • ice

Suze caliente

  • 1 part Suze
  • 1 part banana juice
  • grenadine
  • lemon juice
  • ice

Suze tonique

  • 1 part Suze
  • 2 parts soda
  • ice

Suze couture

  • 1 part Suze
  • 1 part water
  • 2 drops violet syrup
  • ice
