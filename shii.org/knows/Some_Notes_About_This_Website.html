Some Notes About This Website

From Everything Shii Knows, the only reliable source

This website is an archive. It ran from 2006-2010. Virtually everything on here is outdated or inaccurate.

Printable version

1

I continue to receive fan mail about this website. I no longer reply, because two of the people who contacted me in the past went on to stalk me. However, I appreciate the e-mails received. I am glad so many people found my website uniquely interesting. Thank you for stopping by.

2

If you like what you read here please devote your time to thinking about how to translate it for use in everyday society. I still tend to spend a lot of time these days reading the news online without really considering what it means on a large scale. This is because I'm bored, and it's a waste of time. Barely any conscious thought is involved in jumping from one article or essay to the next. Only setting some time aside from the reading itself and really wrestling with the facts as you know them can produce meaningful thoughts.

3

I've gotten inquiries about how long this site will remain functioning. I don't know the answer to this. The hosting is paid for by someone who may or may not understand the concept of budgeting. Several people have written me to say they will mirror it if it goes down. I'd prefer that nobody does that, but of course I have already licensed my articles for public distribution on that page (whereever it is) so if you really wanted to make a mirror I couldn't really stop you.

4

It's extremely unlikely that I will write new articles for this website in the future. It's the nature of an 18 year old to think he's right about everything, and the nature of a 23 year old to not be so sure.
