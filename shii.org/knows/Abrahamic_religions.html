Here is a genealogy of Abrahamic religions by prophets.

  • Adam and Noah
    • Judaism: Abraham
      • Christianity: Jesus
        • Manichaeism: Zoroaster, Buddha
        • Islam: Muhammad
          • Druze: Tāriqu l-Ḥākim
          • Ahmadiyya Muslim Community (Qadiani): Mirza Ghulam Ahmad
          • Bábism: Báb
            • Bahá'í faith: Krishna, Buddha, Zoroaster, Bahá'u'lláh
              • Bahá'ís Under the Provisions of the Covenant: Leland Jensen
        • Mormonism: Joseph Smith
        • Rastafarianism: Haile Selassie I of Ethiopia
        • Creciendo en Gracia: José Luis de Jesús Miranda
        • Church of the Last Testament: Vissarion
      • Sabbatean movement: Sabbatai Zevi
        • Donmeh: Jesus, Muhammad [split: Ismirli, Lechli]
          • Osman Baba: Barukhia/Berechia Russo
            • Frankism: Yakov Leib/Jacob Frank
          • Jakubi: Jacob Querido
    • Mandaeism: John the Baptist
