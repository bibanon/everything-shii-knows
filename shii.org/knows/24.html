Category: Art

The nation is in danger! People have been given too much liberty, and they are using it for evil purposes! Who can save us from this? Not the government! They are too caught up with their foolish, radical notions of freedom for everyone. They are constrained by the necessity of civil liberties. No, we must rely on renegades! Men working in secret, breaking the law and doing terrible things to the violent minority! Only then will they reveal their plots, and the country will be saved!
