One part of Time Cube is actually a fine example of philosophical trouble. A brief translation:

"You are educated stupid - and you have no inkling to just how EVIL you think."
You cannot understand exactly how wrong you are in your beliefs because all of the outside influences on you (society, language) point you towards the wrong beliefs and you can't concieve of anything that lies outside of them.
"'The Word World', imposed by the academic institutions, is synonymous with The Matrix's induced 'Dream World'."
"Word has no inherent value, as it was invented as a counterfeit and fictitious value to represent natural values in commerce."
The true principle of nature is warped by language (i.e. the Sapir-Whorf hypothesis) and the academic institutions which certify an incomplete, human language as equivalent to complete, inerrant reality.

The rest of it is, of course, crazy. Read it yourself: http://timecube.com/
