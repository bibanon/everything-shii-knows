Haklenayasha, also Haklenayaśas.

Those who belong to the lineage of Shariputra meditate on the moon atop Eagle Peak, while those who adhere to the traditions of Haklenayasha transmit the teachings of Mount Kukkutapada.
Nichiren, Rissho Ankoku-ron
When he was seven years old, Haklenayasha went from village to village, and saw people performing illicit cults.
Keizan, Denkoroku
