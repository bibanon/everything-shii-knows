This website is an archive. It ran from 2006-2010. Virtually everything on here is outdated or inaccurate.

Category: But enough about me...

Blindmute Loli is a story about a guy who runs into a blind, mute runaway orphan and the hardships he goes through in his life. The authenticity of this story is impossible to know, but read it yourself and you'll see why it has a devoted following. It is updated every few days, but the updates are posted anonymously to 4chan and not archived anywhere.

Until recently this anonymite's posts were archived by his well-wishers on Wikichan, but that site was shut down due to poor administration and the hard disk was wiped. These threads were restored due to the hard work of yours truly, a kind visitor, and the author himself, and this is the only place most of them can be found on the Internet. Hooray!
