Memphis is an awesome town made of win. They have a lovely minor league baseball stadium, which is cool because I like the minor leagues a lot more than the major leagues.

Places to eat in Memphis:

  • The Rendezvous has the best ribs you'll ever eat. Believe me, I hate ribs. I hate picking stuff off of bones, and I hate getting my hands sticky, and I hate the bottled barbecue sauce they usually come in. However, if you are someone who likes to try new things, consider these ribs a "new thing" separate from the shitty ribs available everywhere else in the United States. THESE ARE NOT YOUR DADDY'S RIBS. ORDER THE RIBS AT THE RENDEZVOUS. I COMMAND YOU.
