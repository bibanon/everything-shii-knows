Here is a list of ideas I had, and why I can't implement them at this time.

  • Recreate Thoreau's log cabin experiment at Walden and live the way he did for a couple of years... making a daily blog of the whole thing.
    • I might do this senior year of college but right now I am a mere freshman and cannot aspire to such great heights. Also, it requires someone who enjoys log cabins more than blogging-- the blog is just the hook.
  • Create a website with a database of sourced facts that could be interpreted to support some political position. Users will be able to add their own facts, comment on or question other people's facts, and group these facts into long lists, which they can use to explain how they create a theory or suspicion.
    • I tried to do this myself, but I'm not a programmer, just a web designer...
  • Print up 1000 copies of Civil Disobedience and hand them out to strangers.
    • Oh man, I wish.
  • Sell fat people slots on a farm as an organic alternative to a gym. They pay you to come work on a farm. Then you sell the food back to them.
  • Make an English clone of Nico Nico Douga.
    • Too profitable for me
